How does the faucet for this large-capacity undersink reverse osmosis water filter work?

The smart faucet comes with an LED indicator and touch controls, allowing you to achieve quantitative water intake without watchful waiting. It can also real-time monitor the TDS value and alert you when it's time to replace filters.

Can I install this RO system myself, or do I need a professional？

The tankless design has fewer pipes and installation steps so you can install this undersink RO system with basic DIY skills, as it includes an easy-to-follow instruction and full accessories.

What impurities does this RO water filter reduce?

The filter is certified by SGS to reduce over 1,000 different impurities including TDS, PFOA, PFOS, Chlorine, Fluoride, Arsenic, Lead, and more, while the auto-sensing LED function ensures water safety.

Does filtration reduce beneficial minerals in the water?

Common RO systems can reduce some beneficial minerals. This undersink reverse osmosis water filter features an alkaline remineralization function that reintroduces beneficial minerals to the water, ensuring a balanced pH for healthier hydration.

What is the water flow rate with this reverse osmosis water filtration system?

This 1200GPD large-capacity undersink RO system delivers a flow rate of about 1200 gallons per day, which is ideal for everyday use. Compared to the traditional tank RO, it achieves faster and more space-savings, while making sure that every drop of water you get is fresh and pure.

Is there a warranty or customer support available for this RO filtration system?

Yes, we provide a 2-year warranty and robust customer support. You can also directly contact our customer service for assistance with any aspect of this RO system without any worries.
